Mech Arena

Mech Arena is a FREE quick-fire competitive robot game with explosive PvP team battles, deep combat tactics, and limitless customization. In this shooter, you can play custom matches against your friends or get them in a team to take down players from around the world in live 5v5 and 2v2 matches.You can also have 5 mechs in your arsenal each with their own slew of uniqueness and many wepaons to add on for thousands of combinations.

A bit a technical vocab

Energy capacity
Each wepaon takes a certain energy capacity the more powerful the weapon the mor energy capacity it takes up. The highest energy capacity for a mech is 32.Energy can be increased with rank upgrade depending on the mech and its energy classification. Brickhouse for example, is unlocked at Rank 5 and have an energy capacity of 18 energy. When you will upgrade it to Rank 6, you will get an energy capacity of 20 and when you fully max it out to Rank 6 level 7 it according to its energy level has an energy capacity of 24.

Ability
Each mech has a unique ability. One can dash away, others create shields. There are a many abilities as there are mechs in the game. Using abilities at key times is critical to winning the game. Abilities such as the one to make barruers that only your team can shoot through can streamline the map and control the flow of mechs allowing for easy chokepoints
Control point clash
Control point clas is a game mode where there is a 5 minute timner. There are also 2-5 control points. For each second you have a control point a point is added to a your teams score. There are 3 ways to win, have the most points when the timer runs out, make your points hit 100 or kill all the enemy mechs.
Medals
There are catagories to get a medal in. Like if you get the most kills, damage or assists. There are gold, silver and bronze medals. These medals are over all your mechs, not limited to one mech like MVP. So it is based on which player got the most kills etc.
